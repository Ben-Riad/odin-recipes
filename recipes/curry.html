Curry massala recipe

Description

Masala rice is a flavorful dish where fragrant, fluffy rice is paired with a rich and spiced masala sauce. The key to preparing it well lies in cooking the rice so that each grain stays separate and light, making it the perfect base for the bold flavors of the sauce. By rinsing the rice thoroughly before cooking, excess starch is removed, preventing stickiness. A quick sautÃ© in a little oil or ghee can enhance the aroma and give the rice a nutty undertone before it is simmered gently until tender.

Once the rice is ready, it becomes the ideal companion for the pre-prepared masala sauce, which is typically a blend of onions, tomatoes, and warm spices such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala. Serving the rice alongside or topped with the sauce creates a balanced dish where the mildness of the rice complements the depth and complexity of the masala. This combination makes masala rice both comforting and satisfying, perfect as a main meal or as part of a larger spread.
